About Us




Freight Movers Express was founded on November 11, 2023 by Pixel, Marcgv2 and with their friends who was obsessed with simulator and mainly trucks. They wanted to create a community of drivers from all over the world who will see each other on the European roads of Euro Truck Simulator 2 and American Truck Simulator

As a company, we currently offer a friendly and fun environment, Drivershub and website, and of course Job tracker, which counts jobs for drivers and sends them to our drivershub
